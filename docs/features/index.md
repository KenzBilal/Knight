# Features

Knight provides a complete suite of tools for B2B sales automation.

## Core Features

### [Lead Discovery](discovery.md)
Find businesses that need your services via Google Maps scraping.

### [Website Audits](audits.md)
Analyze prospect websites with 30+ checks for SEO, performance, security, and more.

### [Email Outreach](email.md)
Send personalized cold emails with AI-generated pitches.

### [AI Pitches](ai-pitches.md)
Generate contextual pitches based on website audit results.

### [Telegram Integration](telegram.md)
AI-powered Telegram conversations for lead qualification.

## Dashboard

### Prospects
Kanban board to manage your sales pipeline:
- **New** - Just discovered
- **Pitched** - Email sent
- **Replied** - Prospect responded
- **Rejected** - Not a good fit

### Inbox
View and respond to email threads from prospects.

### Audits
Review detailed website audit results for each lead.

### Pitches
Manage AI-generated pitches before sending.

## Settings

### [Company Profile](../settings/company-profile.md)
Configure your business information.

### Email Domain
Verify your own domain for email sending.

### Telegram
Connect your Telegram account for lead hunting.

### AI Provider Keys
Use your own API keys (optional).
