# Quick Start Guide

Get up and running with Knight in under 5 minutes.

## Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Go to [knight.com](https://knight.com)
2. Click "Start free"
3. Enter your name, email, and password
4. Click "Create account"

You'll be redirected to your dashboard.

## Step 2: Complete Your Profile

1. Go to **Settings** in the sidebar
2. Fill in:
   - Company name
   - Your website
   - Services offered (comma separated)
   - Calendly link (optional)
3. Click "Save changes"

## Step 3: Run Your First Discovery

1. Go to **Overview** in the sidebar
2. Enter your niche and location (e.g., "plumbers in Austin")
3. Click "Discover Leads"

Knight will:
- Search Google Maps for businesses matching your criteria
- Run a 30+ point website audit on each lead
- Generate personalized AI pitches
- Send cold emails (if configured)

## Step 4: Review Your Leads

1. Go to **Prospects** in the sidebar
2. Review the leads Knight found
3. Drag and drop leads between columns to update their status:
   - **New** - Just discovered
   - **Pitched** - Email sent
   - **Replied** - Prospect responded
   - **Rejected** - Not a good fit

## Step 5: Configure Email (Optional)

To send emails from your own domain:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Email Domain**
2. Enter your domain name
3. Add the DNS records shown to your domain registrar
4. Click "Verify Domain"

## Step 6: Connect Telegram (Optional)

To find leads in Telegram groups:

1. Go to **Telegram** in the sidebar
2. Click "Connect Telegram"
3. Follow the setup wizard
