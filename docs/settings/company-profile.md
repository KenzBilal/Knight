# Company Profile

Configure your business information used in outreach.

## Settings

### Company Name

Your business name as it appears in emails and pitches.

**Example:** "Acme Web Design"

### Your Website

Your business website URL.

**Example:** "https://acmewebdesign.com"

### Services Offered

Comma-separated list of services you provide. This helps Knight generate relevant pitches.

**Example:** "web design, SEO, logo design, branding"

### Calendly Link

Your scheduling link (optional). Included in pitches for easy booking.

**Example:** "https://calendly.com/yourname"

## How This Information Is Used

- **Company name** - Appears in email sender name and signatures
- **Website** - Used to establish credibility in outreach
- **Services** - Helps AI generate relevant pitches
- **Calendly link** - Included in call-to-action buttons

## Tips

- Keep your company name consistent across all platforms
- List your most profitable services first
- Use a dedicated Calendly link for sales calls
